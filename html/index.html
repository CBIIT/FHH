Starting September 6th, 2018, the U.S. Surgeon General's Family Health History Tool has a new home at the Office of Public Health Genomics in the Centers for Disease Control. Users will notice few changes to the tool. Please update the link in your bookmarks to https://phgkb.cdc.gov/FHH.

Using My Family Health Portrait you can:
  • Enter your family health history.
  • Learn about your risk for conditions that can run in families.
  • Print your family health history to share with family or your health care provider
  • Save your family health history so you can update it over time.
